Correct Answer:- The dog’s owner

Under Indian tort law, the dog’s owner is legally responsible for any harm caused by their pet. This is called “strict liability”, meaning the owner must take full care to prevent such incidents. If a dog bites someone, the injured person can file a police complaint for negligence and can also claim compensation in civil court for medical expenses, pain, and suffering. Always keep medical records and witness details, as they help prove your case.
