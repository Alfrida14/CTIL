Correct Answer:- Free and compulsary education

The Right to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 (RTE) is a law that guarantees every child aged 6 to 14 the right to free education. It requires the government to provide schooling and mandates private schools to reserve 25% of seats for disadvantaged children. This law ensures no child is denied education due to lack of fees or documents.
