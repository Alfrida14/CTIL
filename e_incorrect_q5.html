Correct Answer:- File a complaint with the Pollution Control Board

Discharging untreated waste into rivers violates the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974. Affected citizens or groups can file complaints with the State Pollution Control Board, which has the power to inspect, penalize, or even close the factory. They can also approach the National Green Tribunal (NGT) for faster action and compensation.
